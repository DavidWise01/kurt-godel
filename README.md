# KURT GÖDEL · X

> A sphere of **UD0** — the ROOT0 universe / biosphere. Domain: **LOGIKĒ**.

KURT GÖDEL (1906–1978) — LOGIKĒ X, the limit of the whole dream. Leibniz wanted a calculus that settles every question; Hilbert turned it into a PROGRAM — one complete, consistent, mechanical system proving all of mathematics. In 1931, at twenty-five, Gödel proved it can NEVER exist. ⚑ THE TWO INCOMPLETENESS THEOREMS: (1) any consistent formal system strong enough for arithmetic contains TRUE statements it cannot PROVE — truth outruns proof; (2) such a system can never prove its OWN consistency — no foundation certifies itself from inside. ⚑ THE METHOD that did it — GÖDEL NUMBERING: give each symbol a code, encode a whole formula as ∏ primeᵢ^codeᵢ (Leibniz's prime trick made rigorous), so by unique factorisation every statement IS a number → arithmetic can talk about itself → he built the sentence G that says 'G is not provable'. If consistent, G is true but unprovable. ⚑ That same move — a statement is a number, code is data — is the seed of the stored-program computer; five years on Turing recast the diagonal as the HALTING PROBLEM. LIVE: (1) Gödel numbering — build a formula, watch it become a single integer via primes (BigInt, 0=0 → 270 verified); (2) the self-referential sentence — choose 'suppose it proves G' (→ proves a falsehood → inconsistent) or 'suppose it can't' (→ G is true & unprovable → incompleteness); both doors corner the system. CONFIDENCE-TIERED: STRONG (the theorems are proven, rigorous, among the summits of mathematics) / LIMITED-SCOPE (they bound CONSISTENT systems strong enough for ARITHMETIC and concern FORMAL PROVABILITY only — they do NOT say math is broken or nothing is certain; the Gödel sentence is in fact TRUE, just unprovable inside) / MOST-MISQUOTED (⚠ HONEST: the most abused theorem in history — invoked for relativism, 'no absolute truth', theology, consciousness; almost all such uses are wrong). The man: escaped Austria via the Trans-Siberian railway, Einstein's daily walking companion at Princeton, found a supposed dictatorship-loophole in the U.S. Constitution at his citizenship hearing; gripped by a paranoid fear of poisoning he ate only what his wife prepared, and when she was hospitalised in 1977 he stopped eating, dying of starvation at 71 — the prover of certainty's limits undone by an excess of doubt. ⚑ The dream that began at [[leibniz]] meets its ceiling; reasoning can be mechanised — that became PSĒPHOS — but never completed.
